About Me -

" ... when I was at UX Design, nothing could stop me from exploring and learning more about the field... Through my story, I sincerely hope that this can inspire you to continue to look for something or anything that you are passionate about... "
- Read more about my story on Medium


It took me a while to find my path and calling to become a UX Designer. As a previous analyst in diverse business sectors including JPMorgan, the Boston Consulting Group, and an aspiring startup, I deeply believe that a core part of every business is true understanding of users’ perspectives and how to incorporate them into strategic business decisions. 

In this sense, I believe User Experience Design is all about strategic and creative problem-solving in our everyday lives. This is something that I know I love doing, with unstoppable passion. 

Currently, I am studying Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Michigan, and actively seeking a User Experience internship for Summer 2019.

With keen insight, business acumen, strong leadership and analytical communication skills, I am striving to become a User Experience designer who can design for everyone, toward the betterment of our society. 

In my free time, I love reading, writing and sharing ideas with people. You can always find me on Medium and hear more about my journey in finding passion for design. I also enjoy uploading my design works on Dribbble. Check it out!

I'm a firm believer that our society will always be made better through the efforts of those who strive passionately, dare fiercely, and dream wildly. Through my design, this is who I wish to be. Please get in touch if you do too!
